1.A puzzle app dedicated to visual mastery of English verb conjugations. It provides an intuitive way to understand how verbs change based on Tense (Time × Aspect), Sentence Type, and Active/Passive Voice.
2.Multilingual Support:Now fully supports Thai, in addition to Japanese and English.
3.Core Focus:Intentionally excludes adverbs to focus solely on structural transformation, including a manual 3rd-person singular (-s) override for complex subjects.
4.Eco-Friendly & Offline:** Built with AI assistance, but the app itself is 100% standalone. No internet or AI calls during use, ensuring low power and total privacy.
